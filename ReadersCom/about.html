Who We Are

Jordan's Cherished Literary Hub

Readers Bookshop is a distinguished bookstore in Jordan dedicated to fostering a love of reading. Nestled at the 7th Circle Cozmo Centre in Amman, our store is more than a retail space — it's a carefully crafted haven where book enthusiasts of all ages come to discover their next great read.

"We provide an unparalleled literary experience, bridging cultural connections through a diverse selection of Arabic and English titles."

Our dedicated and knowledgeable staff are always on hand to help you find exactly what you're looking for — whether that's an award-winning novel, an educational children's book, a beautifully designed stationery set, or the perfect gift card for a fellow book lover.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Everything we do is guided by a deep belief in the transformative power of literature and the joy of discovery.

Inspire

Every book we stock is chosen to spark curiosity, ignite imagination, and open minds to new possibilities — for readers of every age.

Educate

We champion the role of books in education and personal growth, maintaining a rich selection of titles across academic and non-fiction categories.

Bridge Cultures

Our extensive Arabic and English collections reflect our commitment to linguistic diversity and cultural connection across the Middle East and beyond.

Community

We're more than a shop — we're a gathering place for book lovers. Our space is designed to welcome, delight, and connect the reading community.
